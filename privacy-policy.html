Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, share and protect your personal information — in plain English.

Last updated: 24 April 2026

Home Privacy Policy

At SAS Policy Value Hub Services Pvt Ltd ("SAS", "we", "our" or "us"), your privacy matters deeply. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or use our insurance advisory services, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your data — all in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and the Information Technology Act, 2000.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to recommend, issue and service insurance policies for you:

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact details — name, phone number, email address, city, postal address
  • Personal details — date of birth, age, gender, marital status, occupation
  • Insurance details — preferred cover type, budget, nominee information, existing policy documents
  • Health information (for health and life insurance) — medical history, lifestyle questions required by the insurer
  • Vehicle information (for motor insurance) — registration number, model, year, RC details
  • KYC documents — PAN, Aadhaar (masked), address proof as required by insurers

Information collected automatically:

  • Device and browser information (type, version, operating system)
  • IP address, approximate location (city-level)
  • Website usage data (pages visited, time spent, referral source) via analytics tools
  • Cookies (see Section 6 below)

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected strictly for the following purposes:

  • To recommend suitable insurance plans based on your needs
  • To fetch quotes from insurers and issue policies on your behalf
  • To assist you with claim settlement, policy renewal and after-sales service
  • To send you transactional SMS/email/WhatsApp messages related to your policies
  • To respond to your queries, feedback or grievances
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations under IRDAI, tax and anti-money-laundering laws
  • To improve our website, services and advisor quality (using anonymized analytics)

We never sell your personal data. Your information is treated as a trust — not as a product.

3 Information Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • IRDAI-registered insurance companies — whose policy you choose, to issue and service the policy
  • Authorized service providers — payment gateways, KYC verifiers, cloud storage and IT service vendors bound by strict confidentiality
  • Regulatory authorities — IRDAI, Income Tax Department, courts, or law-enforcement agencies when legally required
  • Professional advisors — auditors, lawyers, and business consultants under confidentiality agreements

We do not share your information with marketing affiliates, lead-sale networks or any unrelated third parties.

4 Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all website communication
  • Restricted access controls for employees on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure cloud hosting with leading providers
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Employee training on data handling and confidentiality

Despite these measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. In case of a data breach, we will notify affected users and the Data Protection Board of India within the timelines prescribed by law.

5 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your insurance policy is active (plus 7 years as required by IRDAI & tax laws)
  • You are an active client receiving ongoing advisory services
  • There is a legal or regulatory requirement to retain the data

After that, your data is either deleted or anonymized for statistical/research purposes only.

6 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (session management, form submissions)
  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)
  • Functional cookies — to remember your preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the site may not work as expected.

7 Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct or update any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to regulatory retention periods)
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing at any time
  • Nominate another person to exercise these rights in case of death or incapacity
  • File a grievance with our Grievance Officer or the Data Protection Board of India

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at contact@policyvaluehub.com. We will respond within 30 days as mandated by law.

8 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information about a minor, please contact us for immediate deletion.

9 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to insurer websites, payment gateways, or partner platforms. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their individual policies before sharing information.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, regulatory or operational changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will always indicate the latest revision. For material changes, we'll notify registered users via email.
